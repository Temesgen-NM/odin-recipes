Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.
It is baked until golden and bubbly, resulting in a hearty and comforting meal enjoyed by many around the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage, cook until browned, then stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and half of the grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish: start with meat sauce, then noodles, ricotta mixture, and repeat until all ingredients are used.
  7. Top with remaining meat sauce and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and the rest of the Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
