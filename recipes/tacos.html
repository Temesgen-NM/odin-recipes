Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, beans, cheese, and vegetables.
They are often garnished with salsa, guacamole, or sour cream, making them a versatile and flavorful meal enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lettuce, shredded
  • Tomato, diced
  • Cheese, shredded (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa or pico de gallo
  • Avocado or guacamole (optional)

Steps

  1. In a skillet, heat some oil and saut√© chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add ground beef or chicken, cooking until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few more minutes until well combined.
  4. Warm taco shells or tortillas in the oven or microwave.
  5. Assemble tacos by filling each shell with the meat mixture, then topping with shredded lettuce, diced tomato, cheese, and any other desired toppings like sour cream, salsa, or guacamole.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious tacos!
